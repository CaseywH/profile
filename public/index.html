Casey Heinemeyer

Emergency/Trauma RN, Web Developer

For the past 13 years I have been in the medical field in various capacities. For the past 8 years I have worked in Emergency and Trauma. I have loved tech and seem to always be an early adopter of the latest inovation. As I began to reach a pinnacle in my career I wanted t find something that challenged me and developed a new skill. With the increase in online courses and training, I turned to web devlopment. In my day to day as an RN, I looked around and saw the need for tech in the nursing/medical field. I took these needs and began to learn and attempt to build out web apps for these ideas.
